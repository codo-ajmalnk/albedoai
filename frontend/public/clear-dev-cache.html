🧹 Clear Development Cache (One Time)

Click the button below to clear all caches and service workers

What this does:

  1. ✅ Unregisters all service workers
  2. ✅ Clears all browser caches
  3. ✅ Clears localStorage
  4. ✅ Clears sessionStorage
  5. ✅ Forces a complete reload

After clicking:

  1. Wait for the success message
  2. The page will redirect to your app
  3. Your dev server will work normally with instant updates! 🎉
Note: Service workers are now disabled in development mode. This is a one-time cleanup. After this, your changes will appear immediately without needing hard reloads!
